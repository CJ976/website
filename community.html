Community Guidelines

We're building a positive, inclusive community. Please follow these guidelines to keep it that way.

Be Respectful

Treat everyone with kindness and respect. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.

Help Others

Share knowledge, answer questions, and support fellow learners. We all started somewhere.

Stay Legal

Only discuss legal, ethical hacking. Never share illegal tools, methods, or encourage malicious activities.

Search First

Before asking, search if your question has been answered. It helps keep discussions organized.

No Spam

Don't spam links, advertisements, or off-topic content. Keep discussions relevant and valuable.

Stay On-Topic

Keep conversations focused on cybersecurity, learning, and helping each other grow.
